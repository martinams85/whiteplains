Frequently Asked Questions*

WHAT IS THE DIFFERENCE BETWEEN LASER HAIR REMOVAL TREATMENTS FOR MEN AND WOMEN?
While it is the same technology, the settings would be different to allow for treatment of more coarse hair. Our technicians have nearly 10 years of experience treating customers of both genders.
DOES HAIR REMOVAL HURT?
Depending on the coarseness of hair, the treatment feels like snapping of rubber on the skin. The laser devices have cooling technology to make treatments more comfortable.
WHAT AREAS CAN BE TREATED WITH LASER HAIR REMOVAL?
We can treat any part of the body except for eyebrows and eyelashes.
